Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 07, 2026

At Color Command, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Color Command mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

The Color Command app is designed to be privacy-first. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal identification information to play the game.

  • Game Progress: Best scores and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: We may collect anonymous data such as device model, OS version, and performance metrics to help us improve the game.

2. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements in the game. AdMob may collect and use certain data (such as your device's advertising ID) to show you personalized or non-personalized ads. You can manage your ad preferences in your device settings.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements.
  • Expo: For framework and application infrastructure.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any data collected. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
